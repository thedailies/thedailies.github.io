Word of the Day

Atavism (adj., AT-uh-vis-mm)

A throwback, when something that was long thought dormant or dead reappears. It is grand and glorious, and celebrated by all—unless it actually *is* the dead reappearing. That's a zombie apocalypse kind of atavism, and nobody likes that (unless it's on television).
