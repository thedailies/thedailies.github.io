What makes a Patek Philippe so valuable

Patek Philippe is an extremely high-end watch manufacturer. In this video from Watchfinder & Co., the host compares a watch that costs $5,500 with a Patek Philippe that costs nearly $100,000. Think the difference is absurd? Perhaps, but Patek Philippe has a design and standard of quality that the $5,500 Omega watch doesn't have:

Patek Philippe's slogan has been "You never actually own a Patek Philippe. You simply look after it for the next generation." With precision and care like that, it's easy to see where that comes from. There's a good reason for that price.
