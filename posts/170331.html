Word of the Day

Epithet (n., EPP-ih-thet)

A term or phrase used to describe someone. It can be descriptive or it can be abusive. If the latter, then the word "hurl" is required to accompany it, as in, "They hurled epithets at the person who called the woman 'Sweet & Low.'"
