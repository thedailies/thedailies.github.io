Word of the Day

Precarious (adj., prih-CARR-ee-us)

Uncertain, barely held in place, in danger of collapsing. The Leaning Tower of Pisa. The leaning tower of cranberry sauce. Arrival times. New Jersey roads. Stomachs after Thanksgiving.
