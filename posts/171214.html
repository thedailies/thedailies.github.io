House encaed in ice near Lake Ontario

It's starting to get cold near Dailies Central, but not as cold as in Webster, New York last year. On a weekend, photographer John Kucko captured some surreal-looking images of a house that was encased in ice. The house was only twenty feet from Lake Ontario, and winds caused the waves to crash against the house, where they froze in eerie stalactites:
