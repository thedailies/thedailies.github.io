Word of the Day

Kayfabe (n., kay-FEEB)

In wrestling, the portraying of staged events as real (and the efforts to keep up the illusion). The term has expanded to include all kinds of illusion/performance art that portrays a false reality. Not that we would know anything about this, of course.
