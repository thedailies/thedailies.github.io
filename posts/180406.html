Word of the Day

Prattle (v., PRAT-ull)

To talk, but to keep talking, especially about something that doesn't matter or has no meaning. It's like a sports talk host, if the sports talk host didn't have anything worth talking about, which is most of the time. You know, they go on and on with some hairbrained idea and they expect you to sit there listening to it. It's ridiculous. I'm gonna change the channel. I'm not gonna give them any more of my attention. It's not fair. My ears don't deserve that.
