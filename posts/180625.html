Word of the Day

Stalwart (n./adj., STAHL-wart)

Someone who is resolute and dependable, or the quality of being dependable. The plow horse. The drummer. The quiet IT guy. The Tood Eaton Award Winner. Your mom (be honest about how much she did for you). Clockwork. Not the MTA (unless it's at being terrible).
