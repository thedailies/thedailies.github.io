Word of the Day

Specious (adj., SPEEEE-schuss)

Sounding like truth but actually being false. It can also mean deceptively appealing. It's the original truthiness, the word that describes all political arguments made to television cameras, as well as well-intentioned suggestions for another's well-being that involve only trivial (if any) changes.
