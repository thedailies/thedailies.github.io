Link of the Day

James White - Signalnoise

James White (a.k.a. Signalnoise) is a child of the 1980s. That is clear from his work, which took a bath in the neon-flavored technology dreams of the 1980s and never stepped out of the tub. And my goodness, is the work good:

(Yes, that is White next to Kurt Russell holding a photo of White's poster.)

You can view more of White's work at Signalnoise.com. Start with the Trials of the Blood Dragon project, which is one of the deepest, best graphic design projects we've ever seen. White is also active on Instagram.
