Word of the Day

Huckster (n., HUCK-stir)

Someone who sells things in the street or someone who uses aggressive, possibly devious means to sell their things. These definitions are not mutually exclusive. WARNING: Hucksters may be dangerous to your wallet and your sanity. The Surgeon General advises a mild intake of hucksters yearly. Please buy responsibly.
