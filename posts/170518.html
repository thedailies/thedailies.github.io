Word of the Day

Missive (n., MISS-iv)

A letter, especially a long one. A missive does not have to be dismissive. In fact, it may be permissive. But this may be remiss; unlike the Swiss, a missive may need to be commissive. To miss this is to be remiss of being derisive.
