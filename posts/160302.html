Word of the Day

Cowed (adj., COWED)

To cause someone to submit with threats of intimidation. Oddly, this does not come from the word "cow" but rather from Scandinavian origin (lthough perhaps that isn't so odd after all; cows aren't exactly intimidating creatures).
