Word of the Day

Facile (adj., FAH-sill)

Easily done, like a good conversation, or superficial, unlike a good conversation. Hrm. That doesn't seem helpful. Running the same play is easy to do but it doesn't lead to any kind of understanding of when and why to deploy it. It's the easy out.
