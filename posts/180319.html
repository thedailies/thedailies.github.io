Link of the Day

Xtend & Climb telescoping ladder

We usually leave product recommendations for other websites, but occasionally, we'll find something that really jumps out to us. We saw something over the weekend that did just that. The Xtend & Climb ladder ia a telescoping ladder. It's made of aluminum frames that fit inside each other, allowing a very tall ladder to collapse into a much smaller profile. The image above shows a fully collapsed ladder. Here's the ladder expanded and in use:

The Xtend & Climb ladder is light, too. A 19-foot ladder weighs about 30 pounds, making it light enough to carry easily. It's not awkward like carrying around an extension ladder or a heavy articulated ladder. The Xtend & Climb can be easily carried with you—and even pulled up if you need to go up another level.

You can purchase an Xtend & Climb ladder at Amazon or find them at one of your local home improvement stores.
