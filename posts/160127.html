Word of the Day

Lassitude (n., LASS-ih-toode)

A state of weariness or lethargy. It is important to note that although "lassitude" sounds magnificent, you need to say it carefully. If pronounced excitedly, it loses all meaning (listening at you, Google pronouncer). This word needs to be pronounced with boredom and defeat, as our dictionary's pronouncer demonstrates so well.
