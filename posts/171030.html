Word of the Day

Supercilious (adj., soo-purr-SILL-ee-uss)

Behaving like you're superior to others, arrogant, haughty. The word comes from the Latin for "eyebrow," so think arching an eyebrow like an evil fairy tale stepmother or Ming the Merciless.
