Word of the Day

Leery (adj., LEAR-ee)

Cautious due to suspicion. A conspiracy theorist. A police captain, in every movie ever. Dana Scully. Someone going 35mph on a highway. People who are happy that Sepp Blatter resigned but don't expect FIFA to get much better.

Jesu, Joy of Man's Desiring in a Forest

To promote a cell phone (!), a company made this elaborate video where a ball rolls down a wooden track in a forest, playing "Jesu, Joy of Man's Desiring" as it goes:

Incredibly, incredibly cool. The amount of work that went into making this is insane, from the crazy long track and the sequence of pitches on it to the intricate system that gets the tremolo notes. And they got the ball to stop perfectly at the cell phone.
