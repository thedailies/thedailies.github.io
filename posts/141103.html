Word of the Day

Delphic (adj.)

Vaguely prophetic and possibly obscure, like an analyst saying that winter is over and spring is coming. Peter Sellers in "Being There" becomes famous by doing exactly this.

Link of the Day

Ruslan Salei Tribute: Life in Sand

Ruslan Salei's fortieth birthday would have been yesterday. Salei was a former NHL defenseman who was killed in 2011 when the airplane taking his hockey team, Lokomotiv Yaroslavl, crashed shortly after takeoff. Salei was captain of the Belarussian national team, which held a charity tournament in his honor. At the opening ceremonies, this sand art was created to commemorate him. It's a stunning five minutes.
