Octopus escapes from boat by squeezing through tiny hole

Octopuses have no bones, which allows them to squeeze through all kinds of tricky, tiny apertures. Here, an octopus escapes a boat by squeezing through a hole the size of a coke can:

The crazy thing is that this is actually a fairly large hole for an octpus. There are other videos and stories of octopuses squeezing through holes 1cm in diameter—only as big as a quarter. Still, it's crazy to see it actually happen.
