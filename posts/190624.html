Word of the Day

Aspiration (n., as-puh-RAY-shun)

The act of breathing or a strong hope of achieving something. The first is a requirement for the second, unless you're talking zombies. If it's zombies, all bets are off.

Link of the Day

City maps turned into organs

For a Swiss science and technology magazine, illustrator and paper artist Katrin Rodegast turned paper maps into organs. The juxtaposition uses highways and waterways to mimic capillaries and nods to how different systems work together. Also, they're pretty awesome:
