Word of the Day

Sporadic (adj., SPOR-ah-dik)

Happening intermittently at irregular intervals. Something that's sporadic can't be formed into a pattern. It wouldn't be like if we started dropping references to something every other week. That'd be too easy. We want to keep you on your toes.
