Link of the Day

Man spends 10 years creating his book in Microsoft Paint

Frank Chimero delivered a popular talk about doing things "the long, hard, stupid way." Pat Hines was way ahead of him. Hines didn't know Photoshop, so he spent ten years illustrating his book in Microsoft Paint. Much of the work was done overnight at a hospital receiving desk. The results are stunningly good:

Hines' labor of love is called Camp Redblood and the Essential Revenge. You can purchase it on Amazon or see more photos from his book at Bored Panda.
