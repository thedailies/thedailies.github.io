Word of the Day

Foment (v., FO'-meant)

To instigate or stir up. The word originally meant to bathe a part of the skin with warm/medicated lotions, which a) makes me wonder why warm/medicated lotions would instigate the skin and b) makes me very glad I don't live in the era when this passed for medicine.
