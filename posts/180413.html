Link of the Day

Marcelo Schultz

Marcelo Schultz is one of our favorite designers despite having an aesthetic that we're not in love with. What we love about Schultz is that he is amazing at the details. Take, for example this design for Nike:

Cool, right? But then look closer:

Schultz does an incredible amount of detail in his images. The three below, like many others, all have painstaking amounts of detail. You have to sweat the small things to be good at what you do, but Schultz does details that few do. It's what separates his work from that of talented imitators. His work has as much realism in its texture as creativity, letting it play in multiple spaces.

You can view more of Schultz's work at his website or on Instagram.
