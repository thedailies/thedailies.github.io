Link of the Day

Trainyard and Flow Free

A quick highlight of two terrific games today. First, Matt Rix's Trainyard is a great puzzle game where you have to get the trains into the station on time, on color, and in the right order:

Similar but less chaotic is Flow Free (iOS and Android), where your goal is to figure out how to make colored paths that don't overlap and fill the entire playing space in the fewest amount of moves possible:

Both games have given Dailies Central a lot of enjoyment and challenge over the years, and we're more than happy to recommend them.
