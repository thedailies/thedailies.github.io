Word of the Day

Braggadocio (n., BRAG-uh-DOE-show)

Cocky, overconfident, annoying boastfulness. Of course, no one ever engages in braggadocio because we're the best and any "boasting" is just telling it like it is. We're not boasting when we say this is and will always be the best website in the world...because it is.
