Word of the Day

Percipient (adj./n., purr-SIP-ee-ent)

Being able to understand things well or someone who is able to see things, especially ones that are ethereal. A percipient person will probably pick up on the foot of this word, as well as what we did at the beginning of the sentence. (They may also see ghosts.)
