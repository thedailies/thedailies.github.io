Phobia List

Do you have paraskavedektatriaphobia? If you do, you've probably been dreading this day (Friday the 13th). Or maybe you have lutraphobia (fear of otters)? Well, you shouldn't; otters are wonderful and harmless creatures. But whatever you have, you can probably find it on the long-running Phobia List. Even if you don't have a phobia, it's fun to see some of the weird entries on the list. (Zemmiphobia, fear of the great mole rat, we're looking at you.)
