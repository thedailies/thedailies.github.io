Link of the Day

1Password

Earlier this year, I bit the bullet and bought a subscription to 1Password. It is the best purchas I've made this year.

1Password is a password manager. It collects and stores all your passwords and other login informtion, auto-fills passwords on command, suggests passwords and rates their strength, and has well-designed apps for mobile, tablet, and desktop. I've used both 1Password and its chief competitor, LastPass; 1Password is by far the superior product. It's secure, fast, and easy to use. The mobile apps in themselves are worth the $3/mo price. The ability to have these features available anywhere is wonderful. If you're tired of remembering 90 passwords, 1Password will make your life much easier.

You can see more details or sign up at 1Password.com.
