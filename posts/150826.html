Word of the Day

Patter (n./v., pat-TURR)

A light tapping sound. Repeated. Rain on an awning.

To move with quick, light steps. Sneaking through the house at night.

To speak quickly and shallowly. A boardwalk street vendor.

The jargon of a group. Military acronyms. Tech terms. Statistic names.
