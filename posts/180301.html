Link of the Day

Tire Rack

Buying tires can be tough: there are lots of options, and it's hard to know which type of tire to get, let alone which brand and specific tire. Fortunately, there's Tire Rack.

Tire Rack rates tires on a series of values, then shows how they stack up to tires in their specific category. Performance on wet surfaces, dry surfaces, snow, and comfort are measured along with how long the tires last and whether you'd recommend them. These results are color-coded, making it easy to judge how good a tire is. It's a good quick overview of what makes tires different and better than each other.

Then, after you've found a tire you like, you can buy it directly from Tire Rack and have it shipped to you or to an installer. It's a nifty setup that often ends up being cheaper than buying directly from the installer or dealer.

Tire Rack gives you the information you need to make smart, frugal decisions about something you'll use heavily. Go check it out.
