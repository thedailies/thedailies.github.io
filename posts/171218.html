Word of the Day

Dapper (adj., DAP-purr)

Dressed neatly and well, stylish, and alert. Typically used to describe men, especially men whose names also begin with "D."

Link of the Day

Black Thought's Hot 97 freestyle

If you want incredible wordplay in your music, you're headed straight for hip hop. The verbal dexterity and intelligence in the bars of the top rappers is amazing, especially when freestyling (off the cuff). Tariq Trotter of The Roots, part of Jimmy Fallon's Tonight Show band, a.k.a. Black Thought, is one of the best of all time. And, after this jaw-dropping 10-minute freestyle on the Hot 97 radio station, he might've solidified his claim as the best:

Note: some adult language. Watch it anyway.
