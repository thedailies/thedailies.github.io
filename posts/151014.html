The games of John Cooney

John Cooney, a.k.a. JMTB02, has been developing games since 2002. Cooney's website says it best: "I aim for short, memorable experiences that push game genres and twist gameplay in wonderful ways." Cooney's games balance a sly sense of humor with a colorful, often cartoonish visual design.
