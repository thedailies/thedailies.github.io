Word of the Day

Casuistry (n., KAZ-oo-ih-stree)

Using clever but not entirely accurate reasoning to make ethical and moral decisions. It's like conspiracy theorizing, but instead of doing it about things that don't matter, you do it about right and wrong. If you don't believe that the internet does this on literally every comment thread, well, then you must be pure evil because reasons.
