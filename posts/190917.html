Word of the Day

Switchback (n./v., SWICH-bak)

A sharp bend in a road or a road/rail that climbs up a mountain by making sharp pivots. Can also refer to the process of going up a hill using a switchback railway. But back to the first definition, it's like a lightning bolt pattern, but mixed with a Z.
