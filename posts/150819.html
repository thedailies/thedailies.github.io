Word of the Day

Sangfroid (n., saun-FWAH)

Composure and coolness, particularly under difficult circumstances or danger. Paul Newman or Steve McQueen. Martial arts masters. Jay Cutler, but for the wrong reasons.

Sometimes, our links don't have to be deeply meaningful or impressive. Sometimes, they just have to be cool.
