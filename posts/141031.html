Word of the Day

Riposte (n.)

Link of the Day

Cmdr. Chris Hadfield's photos from space

Chris Hadfield took 45,000 photos during his time at the International Space Station. Most of them are in the NASA archives but 192 of them are in a new book called "You Are Here: Around the World in 92 Minutes." Some can be viewed online.
