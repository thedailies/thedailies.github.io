Russian Tank Biathlon

There are certain things we try not to cover too much here at The Dailies. Some are just due to overexposure or oversaturation (looking at you, nature videos) but others miss the cut because it's too easy to say, "Look at this crazy thing [crazy person/group] did."

Russia falls into this category. We have links to hilariously ridiculous Russian dashcams that we may post at some time but, again, Russia. Posting Russian dashcams is something Jalopnik does, and we have no intent of mining the same journalism that a Gawker site...aspires to.
