Word of the Day

Lithe (adj., LITHE)

Easily bent and flexible or a person's body that's thin, flexible, and able to move gracefully. Less a straight-line running type and more of an agile, nimble type.

Link of the Day

Giant LEGO cherry blossom tree

At LEGOLAND Japan, a new tree has bloomed. This cherry blossom tree, made for the park's first anniversary, is the largest LEGO cherry tree in the world at over 14 feet high and 17 fet wide. It has 881,470 bricks in it and took over 6,500 hours to assemble. And it's just cool, especially those illuminated LEGO lanterns.
