Word of the Day

Tousled (adj., TOWW-sull'd)

Messy, untidy, unkempt, or disarranged. Though "tousled" is most often used to refer to hair—especially Cosmo Kramer's—it can refer to a wide variety of objects, like rooms, cars, congressional district lines, electrical wiring in a medium-age home, everyone's bedrooms, and the New York Jets.
