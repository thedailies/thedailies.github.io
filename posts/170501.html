Word of the Day

Virtuoso (n., vir-tchoo-OH-so)

Someone who is really, really good at something, especially music. Mozart. Fujimura. Federer. Datsyuk. Kobe. Buckley. Hendrix. Rembrandt. Orwell. Cooke. Malick. SRV. Messi. Day-Lewis. Kanye. Moss. Miyamoto. DFW. Wright. Watterson. Hische. Spielberg.
