Word of the Day

Whippersnapper (n., WIP-purr-SNAP-purr)

A young, inexperienced, and probably overconfident fellow. The kind who will ring your doorbell and expect you to put the candy in the basket for them. Or the kind that will expect that you have never, ever seen a ding dong ditch and that means they can get away with it.
