Link of the Day

Paris

Although The Dailies is a website that chooses to focus on goodness, truth, and beauty, there are times when we must stop to lament instead because the horrors are too much. The attacks in Paris are horrifying—not simply for what they are but because of what they are designed to do and how much they transport us to fourteen Septembers ago.

Tomorrow, we will be back to our traditional content. Life goes on, and so do we. But today, we lament because we must.

Nous souffrons avec vous,
nous pleurons avec vous,
nous sommes un avec vous.
