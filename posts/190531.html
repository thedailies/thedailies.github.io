Link of the Day

Goodbye, Three Crazy Ladies

Today is the last day for the best gas station food in Pennsylvania. Three Crazy Ladies is closing.

Five years ago, three PA Main Line soccer moms decided to open a restaurant. They found a spot inside a Sunoco gas station at a busy intersection near some suburban Pennsylvania corporate parks and began serving breakfast and lunch. Being inside the gas station helped keep the overhead costs low, which let them offer delicious, stunningly full meals at a normal lunch price. They quickly gained a reputation as the best takeout lunch shop in the area. A meal from 3CL was known to be healthy, tasty, and usually enough for three meals.

They announced their closing earlier this year. None of them ever expected to become this beloved. It was something to try, an opportunity to have fun working together and sharing with others. But it took a lot of their time and energy, and they need those things now to take care of their families. But they've gone out in style, retiring a meal a day and celebrating on their Facebook page.
