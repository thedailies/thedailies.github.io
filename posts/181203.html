Word of the Day

Expurgate (v., ECK-spur-gate)

To remove potentially unsuitable material from a book before publication, especially because it might be objectionable. Usually not for well-intentioned, great reasons.

Link of the Day

Group attempts to recreate the Titanic in a video game

A group of video game developers are creating a game set in the Titanic, and as part of it, they're creating a fully explorable Titanic using the Unreal Engine 4. One YouTuber has posted a video with a walkthrough of the demo (starts around 1:40; some adult language after 16:00):

The crazy thing? This is only 6% of the actual ship. There is much, much more to create.

You can download the demo and explore yourself or contribute to the building effort over at TitanicHG.com.
