Link of the Day

Andre Rieu plays Sirtaki

The thing I love about Andre Rieu is how he subverts your expectations about classical music with joy. The pomp and pageantry is on full display but this is fun. Rieu is the ringleader but also effortlessly shares the stage with others. We're brought into the music, into his enjoyment of it, and it becomes a treat.
