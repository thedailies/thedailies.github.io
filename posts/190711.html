Word of the Day

Gloaming (n., GLOW-ming)

Twilight or dusk, when the sun is hidden but not gone, its rippled effects lingering on the sky.

Aerolux flower bulbs

The Aerolux Light Corporation manufactured some interesting light bulbs during the middle of the 20th century. The filaments were designed into shapes of flowers, birds, and other things, and the bulbs were filled with gasses that would change color as they glowed. Here are some of the results:
