Word of the Day

Traipse (v., TRAYPS)

A hike (by miles):

Miles 0–1: Yay! This will be fun!

Miles 2–3: Oooh, nature! We should do this more often.

Miles 4–5: This was good but I'm glad we'll be wrapping up soon.

Miles 6–7: Are you really sure you know the directions?

Miles 8–9: My feet. My stomach. Commencing traipsing...

Miles 10+: Never again.
