Mollycoddle (v./n., MAHL-lee-CAHD-ull)

To overindulge, overprotect, and pamper someone, or a man who has been mollycoddled. We're not talking a normal level of helicopter parenting here; we're talking absurd levels of babying and indulgence, attention to your every desire helicopter parenting.
