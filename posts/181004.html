The chills you get when intensely emotionally awoken, a thrill, strong feelings of excitement, the physical response of an awoken soul.

It's directed by Rian Johnson (the guy who made the "Star Wars" movie a lot of people hated), stars David Strathairn and Sissy Spacek, is dramatic and poignant, and is both at odds with the song's lyrics and an interesting extension of them.
