Word of the Day

Provincial (adj./n., pro-VINN-shull)

Relating to a province, or limited in scope or awareness. When referring to people, the lack of awareness can extend into poor manners or non-sophistication. It would be like calling someone of this kind "an provincial." (We'll let you figure it out.)
