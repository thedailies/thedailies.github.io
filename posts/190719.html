Happy Friday

LEGO Saturn V rocket

A couple years ago, LEGO introduced a kit for the Saturn V rocket. Appropriately, it has 1969 pieces and is to scale. It's a stunningly good recreation, complete with breakaway segments and a lunar lander that fits into the rocket. It'll give you hours of enjoyment. Go buy one and put it together.
