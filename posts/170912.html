Word of the Day

Cicatrix (n., SICK-uh-tricks)

The scar of a healed wound, a reminder of what has been and what is now. Perhaps an admonition to do better. Perhaps a portal to regret. Perhaps a reminder that time has started and we are now walking in it. The enduring, the keepsake, the trigger to emotions.
