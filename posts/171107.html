Word of the Day

Oeuvre (n., OOVE-ruh)

The sum total of an artist's work. It's a French word, because you graduate to hifalutin language only when you produce enough quality work. You are then qualified to drink tea with your pinky out.

Link of the Day

Film Loop podcast

It's rare that we get to feature our friends' work on The Dailies, but today, we get that treat.

Film Loop is a podcast about movies from different generations. Each episode, hosts Victor De Anda (GenX-er) and Darian Davis (Millenial) pick movies from their respective generations and discuss them. It's a great idea for a show. Movies are something we share with others, and it's fun to hear the two cohosts share their experiences and perspectives.

You can subscribe on Apple podcasts or find other options and old episodes at filmloopshow.com.
