Word of the Day

Cantankerous (adj., can-TANGK-er-us)

Obstinately disagreeable, quarrelsome, and hard to manage. This cat. Kanye West. A New Jersey driver who has endured a 30-minute traffic jam. Dez Bryant. A mama bear who finds out you took her cubs. Chris Christie during a press conference. Walter Sobchak.
