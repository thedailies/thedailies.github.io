Word of the Day

Transient (adj., TRAN-zee-ent)

Fleeting, something that passes through an area but leaves, something that doesn't stay the same but goes away. Someone that you catch up with after they're gone and leveled up. Something that wasn't meant to stay forever. Something that's migrated. Something that has accepted its nature of being shadows and dust.
