Word of the Day

Supererogate (v., SUU-purr-ERR-oh-gate)

To do more than is required or expected, like a kid who makes his mom breakfast in bed and then makes her tea by using the fly swatter to strain the tea leaves. (True story, though it didn't happen to anyone at Dailies Central.)
