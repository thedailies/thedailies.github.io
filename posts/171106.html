Link of the Day

Line Rider - In The Hall of the Mountain King

Line Rider is a tool/game/diversion that allows you to draw a path for a small virtual tobogganer while a song plays. You can load a track into the editor and start drawing, matching the mood of the song to the action on screen. (Give it a whirl. It's fun.)

Recently, a user named Doodle Chaos created his or her first (!) Line Rider drawing, set to Edvard Grieg's "In The Hall of the Mountain King." It is absolutely fantastic:

Bravo, Doodle Chaos. Hope to see more from you in the future. If you enjoyed this, you might enjoy exploring the imaginative realm of Line Rider art.
