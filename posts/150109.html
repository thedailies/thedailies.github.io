Word of the Day

Hankering (n., HANK-er-in')

A strong desire for something. Bruce Dickinson had a hankering for more cowbell. Maverick and Goose had a hankering for speed. Some people have a hankering for fried chicken on Fridays. Allen Iverson did not have a hankering for practice.

Link of the Day

Hubble Space Telescope turns 25

The Hubble Space Telescope turns 25 years old this year, and, to celebrate, NASA has released a new image of the famous "Pillars of Creation" photo. A moderately-sized version is above; versions of all sizes are available on the Hubble website.

They've also released a new photo of the Andromeda galaxy that's worth seeing. (Note: the largest file is over 200MB, so don't try to view it in your browser.)
