Detailing a car that had been sitting for nearly 40 years

A NJ doctor bought a car in the late 1960s, drove it for a couple years, and then left it in a garage since then. Larry from AMMO NYC worked with the doctor's son to visit NJ and do a quick detail/restoration on the car, surprising the dad. (They would detail the car more fully later, but they wanted the surprise.) They filmed that process for the classic 1969 Mercedes 280 SL:

As one of the first comments notes, "When doctor S moves his jacket back to put his hands on his hips... Thats internationally recognized as the old man sign language for 'Well gosh darn, I'm impressed...'"
