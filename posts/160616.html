Word of the Day

Panacea (n., pan-uh-SEE-uh)

A cure-all. A solution for all diseases or problems. Often sold by huckster salesmen, a panacea is always too good to be true. It usually tastes sugary. But it's wonderful! It will make your wildest dreams come true! You should totally take it!
