Word of the Day

Aver (v., uh-VARE)

To affirm or state to be the case. To assert as a fact, in a court of law. To declare, or do declare (with a Foghorn Leghorn accent).

(Note: The Dailies does not endorse averring facts in a court using a Foghorn Leghorn accent and takes no responsibility for any actions that might result from that kind of ill-advised decision.)
