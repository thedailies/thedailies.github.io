Word of the Day

Railroad (n./v., RAIL-road)

Ok, we know you know what this word means as a noun. But did you know that it's also a verb? And that it can mean to rush something through in order to avoid careful consideration? Or to convict someone on trumped-up charges? You didn't, and that's because you railroaded your vocabulary homework so you could play StarCraft.
