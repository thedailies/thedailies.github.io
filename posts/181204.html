Word of the Day

Impudent (adj., IM-pew-dent)

Not showing respect for another person, whether through brashness, offensiveness, or obliviousness. Often a trait of youngsters and upstarts; hopefully not a trait of a leader.

Blue Note Records' album covers

Vox has a great video introducing us to the history of Blue Note Records' album covers from the 1950s and 60s. The legendary jazz label didn't just create a distinctive sound; they also made some of the best album art for any genre at any time.
