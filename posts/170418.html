Word of the Day

Rummage (v., RUM-mijh)

A disheveled search pattern often done in attics. Rummaging often involves tossing things willy and spreading them out nilly and generally increasing the messiness of the area while trying to find the one missing thing. When people say they're rummaging, they're being honest. If they say they are looking for something, they are deceiving themselves.
