Link of the Day

Art of the Title

The title sequence of a TV show or movie has a surprising amount of work to do. Not only does it have to run through the usual information (title, director, producers, actors, etc.), it also has to keep the user's interest without dialogue or other plot development. And, if possible, it needs to set the tone for the rest of the show.

The Art of the Title highlights the best title seequences, ones that manage to do all those things with an artistic flair. Whether something from the 60s like Saul and Elaine Bass or something more modern like Guardians of the Galaxy: Volume 2, Art of the Title catalogs them, gives some background on how they were made, and sometimes has an interview with the designers too.

The Art of the Title illuminates the world of a little-known but surprisingly interesting part of art. Go check it out.
