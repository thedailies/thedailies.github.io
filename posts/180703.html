Link of the Day

Phrase Shift

One fun little game we've enjoyed recently is Phrase Shift. In it, you're presented a clue and asked to drag the words so their letters form a word that solves the clue. The clues are good, and you start to get used to them after a few puzzles. (There are a lot of puzzles, too.) It's a fun workout for your vocabulary.

Phrase Shift is free on the iTunes store and the Google Play store, and costs $2.99 on Steam.
