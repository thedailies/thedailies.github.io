Word of the Day

Gentrify (v., JENN-trih-fi)

To refine or polish something, but often to the level of middle-class tastes. Usually used to describe neighborhoods that have turned over.

Link of the Day

Rubiks cube portraits of celebrities

Over the weekend, we spotted this video online:

This is the work of Giovanni Contardi, a Rubik's Cube artist who's created many large-scale portraits using hundreds of the classic toys. If working with a public gallery, Contardi usually places his creations in a 5' x 5' frame (like the RDJ one). But other times, he'll scramble everything once he's done.

You can see more of Contardi's work (including a giant 1500-cube portrait of Ellen DeGenneres and Contardi solving a cube in 6.69 seconds) by following him on Instagram.
