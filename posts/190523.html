Word of the Day

Mordent (n., MORE-dent)

A quick alteration of a note where the note immediately below the first note is played. It's like a trill, but it goes down. As they say, if it's not Baroque, fix it.

Bruce the Eagle photographed perfectly symmetrically

Sometimes, you're in the right place at the right time. Amateur photographer Steve Biro happened to be in the right place to catch Bruce the Eagle swooping down at the Canadian Raptor Conservancy, grabbing this stunning photo:
