Link of the Day

Sara Hendren's website

Sara Hendren has one of the most interesting homepages I've seen. After learning that she's a professor whose research focuses on disability, art, and engineering, the website makes more sense—it's well designed for exactly those things—but the homepage is really fun to watch. Go take a look, and then explore some of the things she's done. (The lectern and accessible icon project are especially good.)
