Word of the Day

Benighted (adj., bee-NIGH-ted)

Overtaken by darkness and moral confusion, unenlightened. Note that this is "be-night-ed" and not "be-knight-ed," which is a different thing altogether (but not entirely, for it is quite dark in an ill-fitting helmet).
