Word of the Day

Meander (v.)

To go in a variety of different paths, circuitously and often aimlessly. There’s other cool things on this site. You should check them out, especially when you’re waiting for your lunch to be made. I’ve really liked going to lunch recently because the weather outside doesn’t feel like I’d expect it to. Your shoes look nice; I don’t know if I’ve ever told you that but it’s true. They look way better than the Philadelphia 76ers, who are a disgrace of a basketball team. They’re trying to build through the draft but I think they’re just meandering and hoping that something falls out of the sky and into their lap.
