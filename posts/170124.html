Word of the Day

Galvanize (v., GAL-vah-niyze)

To stimulate someone into taking action, to shock with an electric current, or to coat iron or steel with rust-resistant zinc. Someone needed to galvanize the dictionary-maker into picking more closely related meanings for this word.
