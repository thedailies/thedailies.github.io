Word of the Day

Furtive (adj., FURR-tivv)

Secretive and trying to avoid attention, possibly from doing something that should not be done. Guilt fidgeting. Looking askance. Not responding when being asked a question. Running and hiding. How you behave when you know that you are a bad boy and should not have peed on the carpet.
