Word of the Day

Withies (n./adj., WITH-ees)

A long, flexible twig which can be fashioned into a rope or band, or the quality of being tough but flexible, like the principals we say we want but not the memories we have of the principals we had.

Link of the Day

GeoGuessr

GeoGuessr is a free, five-minute online game where you have to guess where a place is based on a photo of it. When you start a game, you'll be placed on a random spot in Google Maps' Street view, like so:

...and your job is to guess where in the world it is. You can move around inside the street view to get clues about where it might be. Once you think you know where it is, click the map, then click Make Guess to see how close you are.

Each round is five questions long. You can play it individually or against someone else. Happy guessing!
