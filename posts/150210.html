Link of the Day

Yearbook

Yearbook, a five-minute animated film written and narrated by Bernardo Britto, is something that has stuck with me for a couple weeks since I've seen it. It's a film you should watch a few times, until you start picking up on the little details in the background that mirror what the narrator vocalizes. And the central message (which I won't spoil) is a terrific example of the "look for and enjoy things, especially small details" theme we discussed yesterday.
