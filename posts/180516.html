Word of the Day

Corpuscle (n., core-PUSS-ull)

A single, discrete cell or part of something else, possibly light. Not related to "crepuscular." You can make a sentence with both, though: This crepuscular period of LeBron James in Cleveland is marked by the isolation-heavy plays and team defense breakdowns, as a collection of corpuscles searches for a full body.
