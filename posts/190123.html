Word of the Day

Philippic (n., fill-IP-ick)

A bitter, vicious verbal attack on someone. Originally referred to Demosthenes of Athens' speeches against Philip of Macedon, but now does not have to refer to Philips or Macedonians. Because that's what American freedoms do to the English language.
