Word of the Day

Owamagbe Odighizuwa (DE, oh-wuh-MAH-bey oh-DIG-ee-ZU-wuh)

Defensive End, born in Columbus, of Nigerian descent, formerly of UCLA and now of the New York Giants, and the most unpronounceable name in the NFL draft this year.

Link of the Day

Spurs vs. Clippers, Game 7

We don't feature sports much on The Dailies, and that's partly a choice. There's more than enough digital ink spilled on sports without us adding our commentary.

But once in a while, there's something worth highlighting. Saturday night's Game 7 between the San Antonio Spurs and Los Angeles Clippers was on at Dailies Central and wow, was it ever good:

My goodness. That was a championship-level game (from an equally good series) in the first round. It's a shame one of these teams had to lose. Hopefully this isn't the last game for Duncan and Ginobili, but if so, that's an incredible but heartbreaking way to go out.
