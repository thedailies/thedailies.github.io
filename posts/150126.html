Word of the Day

Bowdlerize (v., BOUGHED-lur-ize)

To remove offensive material from a work. In the early 1800s, Thomas Bowdler published an edited version of Shakespeare with certain parts removed. The word usually has a negative connotation because why would you think you could out-edit Shakespeare? A rose by any other name would not sound.
