Link of the Day

Ostriches do not eat well

An ostrich died at the London Zoo in 1942. This is what they found in its stomach:

Contents: a lace handkerchief, a buttoned glove, a length of rope, a plain handkerchief (probably a man's), assorted copper coins, metal tacks, staples and hooks, and a four-inch nail. The nail, they concluded, was probably the cause of its death.
