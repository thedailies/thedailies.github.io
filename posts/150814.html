Link of the Day

Lightyear.fm

This is incredibly cool. Radio broadcasts leave earth at the spead of light, so if you go further and further from Earth, the songs you'll hear are older and older. Lightyear.fm took this idea and made it into a website. So in the example above, you'd have to go 10.2 light years away from Earth to hear something from 2005.

Seriously: this is incredibly cool.
