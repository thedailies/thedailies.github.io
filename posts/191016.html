Word of the Day

Kook (n., KOOK)

A strange, crazy, eccentric person. Sometimes a kook lives in a cabin in the woods. Sometimes they live in a creepy house at the edge of town. Sometimes they walk among us. But they are always a step away from the normal rhythm of the world.
