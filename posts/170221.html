Bruce Springsteen pulls kid out of crowd to play "Growin' Up"

At a recent concert in Brisbane, Australia, Bruce Springsteen spotted a sign near the stage that read, "Missed school, in the [expletive] now, can I play Growin' Up with you?"

"Do you know the song?" Springsteen asked the teenager who made it.

"You know it on guitar?" A yes. "Well, come on up!"

Note: the sign (with the expletive) is visible in the first two seconds.
