Rousseau plays Liszt's La Campanella

Rousseau is a YouTuber who plays a digital piano that's hooked up to a reactive visualizer to create fascinating videos. Recently, as a thank you to his 100,000 subscribers, he uploaded a video of him playing Franz Lizst's "La Campanella":

The comments section is grat, especially for them pointing out that Lizst and Paganini were suspected for their otherworldly keyboard talents. To pull this kind of piece off this well is some impressive technical skill. The visuals are almost unnecessary, but they help to illustrate how good this is.
