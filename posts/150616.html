Word of the Day

Plenipotentiary (adj./n., PLEN-ih-puh-TEN-she-air-ee)

Given full powers to make decisions and do things, or a person given this authority (often a diplomat in a foreign country). Also, a word that has an impressive amount of syllables and sounds funny when you put the emphasis on other syllables.
