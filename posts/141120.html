Word of the Day

Ensconce (v.)

To settle yourself in a secure, comfortable place, or to firmly place or conceal something in a secure place. What you'll do on the weekend when you're going to read a book, or what you'll do with your Christmas gifts in a month.

Link of the Day

Buffalo gets four feet of snow

Buffalo got hit with four feet of snow earlier this week. A giant wall of snow (pictured above) dumped a massive amount of snow on the city. (And they're supposed to get 18–36in more this weekend.) Time magazine compiled a variety of photos showing how much snow is there from various Instagram accounts around the city.

There's also a time-lapse video that shows the storm moving in and dumping all the snow on the city, which is also worth watching.
