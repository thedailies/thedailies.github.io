Word of the Day

Buffalo (n./v./adj.)

A large plains animal with massive shoulders, a big head, horns, tiny legs, and a small tail. A city in western New York. Something that's like that large plains animal or from that city. To confuse or fluster. And all this lets us make the most confusing sentence in the English language, Buffalo buffalo Buffalo buffalo buffalo buffalo Buffalo buffalo. You're welcome.
