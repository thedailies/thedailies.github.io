Word of the Day

Stile (n., STYLE)

A set of steps that allows humans but not animals to cross over a fence or wall. Stile comes from the Latin word for "post" or "pointy thing", as does the word "style." Remember, kids: there is—literally—nothing more stylish than a pointy stick.
