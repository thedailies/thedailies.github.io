Word of the Day

Sward (n., SWARD)

A small grassy area, like a meadow or lawn. An area like this in Dallas can cause conspiracies. It can be the site of family get-togethers. It can be a haven in the middle of a concrete jungle. It's a bevy of beautiful things, so we gave it the most boring, pointed, hideous name ever. English!
