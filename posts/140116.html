Word of the Day

Garrulous (adj.)

William Henry Harrison was the ninth president of the United States of America. He was also president for the shortest amount of time. His inauguration took place on a rainy day but that didn't stop him from delivering at garrulous 100-minute speech and walking much of the way in the procession. Naturally, he got pneumonia and died a month later. John Tyler became president upon Harrison's death.
