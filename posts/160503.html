Word of the Day

Lucubration (n., LOO-kyew-bray-shun)

Deep study or thought, or a writing that comes out of a period of study and thought. Lest you think that this is a good thing, our dictionary assures us that these writings are often viewed as overelaborate, overlong, and pedantic. Don't think, kids. Do or do not. There is no lucubration.
