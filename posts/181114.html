Word of the Day

Cosseted (adj., KOSS-eh-ted)

Pampered and cared for in an overindulgent way. Not just building a doghouse but decorating it like a home, putting a motion-activated water fountain for it, and making sure it had steak cuts to eat.

Eric Chien's world championship-winning magic tricks

Every three years, there is a world championshp for magicians. The judges are other magicians. Eric Chien, a close-up magic specialist, won it this year with his "Ribbon" demonstration. It's quite good:

There's an incredible amount of tricks packed into that routine, all strung together well, all similarly hard to believe. And to do them in close-up, where it's harder to pull off an illusion, is impressive. The man is good. Congratulations on a well-earned win, sir.
