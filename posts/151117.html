Word of the Day

Turgid (adj., turr-JID

Excessively ornate in style, or distended and swollen with fluid. Someone who wears too many pieces of flair. Someone who just chugged a half gallon of milk. Baroque architecture. Effie Trinket. A Muse song. A Wes Anderson film.
