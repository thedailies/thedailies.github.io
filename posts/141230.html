Word of the Day

Meretricious (adj., mare-eh-TRISH-us)

Something that sounds possible but isn't actually true, like vaccines causing autism. Attractive but fake. It can also mean something that attracts the wrong kind of attention. There's also a third meaning but that's closer to the origin of the word and not its common usage in business publications.
