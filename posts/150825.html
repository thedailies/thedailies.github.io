Link of the Day

Explore Mars from your computer

NASA has unveiled a couple webapps that let you explore Mars right from your computer. The first, Experience Curiosity, lets you drive the Mars Rover around a panoramic surface of the Red Planet. It's fun, and going full-screen on a big monitor is even better.

The other, Mars Trek, allows you to view detailed images from Mars (like the one shown above) and analyze data collected from the planet. The images are really detailed, more than you would think.

All in all, a fun coffee break or lunchtime exploration. Thanks NASA!
