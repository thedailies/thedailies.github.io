Word of the Day

Astringent (n./adj., uh-STRINN-jent)

Something pungent that draws things together or constricts them. Normally, drawing things together is a good thing. Your grandmother will draw the family together for Easter, and that's great. But an astringent is much sharper and more severe, and that's not like your grandmother. It is like the smell of her Baked Octopus, but that tends to drive people away.
