Word of the Day

Calyx (n., KAY-licks)

On a flower, the cuplike structure that the sepals (the green things below the petals) make. On anything else, a cuplike structure for holding things.

Link of the Day

The Boxcar Lilies - If I Needed You

Townes Van Zandt's "If I Needed You" has a curious origin. Van Zandt was asleep at the home of some friends in Nashville, dreamed that he was a country singer, woke up, and wrote down the song from his dream in its entirety, then went back to bed. The song has remained unchanged.

It's been covered a number of times, notably by Doc Watson, but this version by the Boxcar Lilies, off their excellent "Sugar Shack" album, is terrific:
