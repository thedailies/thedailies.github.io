Word of the Day

Indulgence (n.)

Giving into a temptation, especially to enjoy it. To some, eating Chick-Fil-A does not fall into this category. (Also, in the Roman Catholic Church, a lessening or covering of punishment to come for sin. Sales of these started the Reformation.)
