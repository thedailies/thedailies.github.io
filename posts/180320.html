Word of the Day

Besmirch (v., bee-SMURCH)

To damage the reputation of someone or something, or to dirty, soil, or stain. Look, Staind was a terrible rock band, but even with the same songs, a group called "Smirchd" just sounds better. And maybe we wouldn't have gotten Aaron Lewis' country album. This is not besmirching. This is hard fact.
