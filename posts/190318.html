Link of the Day

Vivian Mayer's color street photography

I would have a hard time not showing 150,000 photos that I've taken, but that's what Vivian Mayer did. A nanny and self-taught photographer, she snapped photos of city life but hid them until an estate sale revealed them after her death. Although there's been a documentary and interest in her black-and-white work, her color photography hasn't been as prominent. Colossal had a small selection of these photos:

You can see more color photos over at Colossal or buy a book of it. There's also a site that features her black and white photos.
