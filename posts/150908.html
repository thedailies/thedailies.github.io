Word of the Day

Debuccalization (n., dee-BUCK-cull-ih-ZAY-shun)

Get ready. This word is a doozy—but it's not much trouble to understand it. Debuccalization simply means the way we drop the hard sound at the end of a word, like when we say "mountain" and it sounds like "mao-nnnnnn." It's not good but it's what we do.
