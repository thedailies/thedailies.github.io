Word of the Day

Inoculate (v., ih-KNOCK-you-late)

To protect against something harmful by introducing a small amount of it as a vaccine or, more generally, to safeguard against harm. We're not talking going full Face/Off here. You don't want to replace everything, just a little bit, because what doesn't kill you makes you stronger. This includes listening to the Bruce Willis album of the same name.

Link of the Day

Ural Music Night rebranding

We have a small theme week going, but we're going to interrupt it. Ural Music Night is a music festival in Russia that just updated its branding. The old branding was solid, but the new one is fantastic, combining a torn paper aesthetic with a variety of logo styles for each type of music featured at the show. The intro video is a good overview:

There's shades of the Tribeca Film Festival here, but remixed in a way that feels appropriate to a music festival in Russia. The posters are good; the t-shirts are brilliant. Even if they're going to be unbelievably hard to make, they're fantastic.

You can see more of the design over at Brand New.
