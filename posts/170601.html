Word of the Day

Discursive (adj., dih-SCURR-sieve)

Rambling, covering a wide range of topics, like when you start talking about food but it then turns to politics and then someone drops a sports metaphor in the middle before you both start talking about the best book you've read recently. We wouldn't know. It's not anything that's happened to us. Ever.
