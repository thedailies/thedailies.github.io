Link of the Day

Aliaksei Zholner's paper organ

This is crazy cool. Aliaksei Zholner made a working organ entirely out of paper (and a balloon). Have a listen:

The organ has 18 keys (all tuned; seriously, this is nuts) that play notes on the reeds. Also, Zholnar says that this is too hard to produce. If a man can make something this intricate and cool but says it's too hard? It's waaaay too hard. What an instrument.
