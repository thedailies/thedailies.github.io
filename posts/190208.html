Word of the Day

Ingenue (n., ON-jenn-ooh)

An innocent, naive girl or young woman, especially in a play or work of fiction. It comes from a word meaning "without fault," NOT the one about being "brilliant, like a genie." But your name can be Jeanie. And you can be glowing.
