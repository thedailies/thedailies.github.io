Word of the Day

Thixotropic (adj., THIK-so-TROP-ick)

You know how a lot of gels or gel-like substances will soften when stirred or shaken? Thixotropic ones do that but then go back to being more solid when they sit. Some custards are good examples of this. Toothpaste is not.
