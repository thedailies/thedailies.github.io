Word of the Day

Meticulous (adj., mih-TICK-you-luss)

Demonstrating extreme carefulness about details or being very precise. A proofreader. An animal grooming itself. Dancers. A fire twirler. Nick Saban. Talking about near-secrets. Good craftsmen.

Link of the Day

Infectonator

One shared element for all of our games this week is short levels coupled with a moderate-length game. Infectonator might be the best example of this.

The premise is simple: infect the world with zombies. In Infectonator, you accomplish this goal in two ways: clicking to spread a virus to nearby subjects, turning them into zombies who do zombie things (and sometimes turn the others into zombies) or deploying super-zombies. The levels are short—some of the early ones can be played in 15–20sec—but there's a lot of them and a decently challenging set of objectives for each level. It's a nice balance of fun, luck, and skill.

Side note: Infectonator is also available for mobile devices. From what I've heard, it works well on mobile as well.
