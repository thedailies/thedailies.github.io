Link of the Day

Smashed porcelain cans by Lei Xue

In a thought-provoking blend of contrasts, Chinese artist Lei Xue sculpted porcelain to look like smashed aluminium cans, decorating them by hand with motifs from Ming Dynasty.

More of Xue's work can be seen at Martina Detterer Gallery.
