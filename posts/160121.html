Word of the Day

Exhume (v., ehks-ZOOM)

To remove from a grave or, more generally, to bring something back into consciousness after some time. We tried to think of an illustration that contrasted with "chicken-and-egg" things but everything ended up being disturbing. That's what happens when you exhume something. You hear that, remixers?
