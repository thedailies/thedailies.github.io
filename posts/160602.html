Word of the Day

Equanimity (n., eh-KWAH-nim-ih-TEE)

The quality of being calm and even-tempered. Composure. Occasionally related to phlegmatic. Sometimes related to Ryan Gosling. Usually not related to redheads. Always related to Roger Federer (even in angry moments).
