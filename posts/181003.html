Word of the Day

Voluminous (adj., voll-OOM-ih-nuss)

Massive, big in size, looooong, or capable of being written down at length. Think the electrical codes that electricians have to learn or the amount of textbooks that doctors acquire in their training.

Epicurious' Price Points: a meat expert guesses between cheap and expensive meats

Epicurious has an ongoing series called "Price Points" where experts in a specific type of food are asked to taste test foods and guess whether they're cheap or expensive. The concept is decent, but the execution is the best part of the show.
