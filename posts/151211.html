Word of the Day

Bloc (n., BLOCK)

A group of countries, parties, or people that share a common purpose or goal. Why not use "block" like normal people? Well, normal people are a bloc, and thus one needs to do things different to stand out. This is also what created Lady Gaga. See where that got us.
