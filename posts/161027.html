Word of the Day

Imprimatur (n., im-pruh-MAH-toore)

Approval, officially, like with a seal or some kind of fancy symbol. Originally used to refer to a Roman Catholic Church's blessing to print a book, it can now be used to refer to anything that is approved. Pope sumus.
