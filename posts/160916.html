Link of the Day

Font Squirrel

There's an overabundance of free fonts these days thanks to the internet, and finding a good one can be a daunting task. There's a ton of sites that have free fonts but they're difficult to sort through or require login to download a font.

Font Squirrel is the answer to this problem. The site curates its fonts and features high-quality free fonts. Also, it's easy to sort through the fonts and find what you're looking for. They also include a webfont generator so you can use the fonts in your website. It's the best font searching website for most people, and it should be the first place you turn when you need a font.
