Word of the Day

Rapscallion (n., rap-SKALL-yun)

A mischievous person. The artful dodger. Spanky, Alfalfa, and Buckwheat. Toddlers. Hobbits, to Gollum. Dean. Anyone who twirls a mustache. Pirates.
