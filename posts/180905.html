Word of the Day

Exonerate (v., egg-ZAUN-uh-rate)

To officially release someone from blame for something wrong, often after some kind of legal proceedings, or to declare someone free from responsibility. The opposite of egging someone on.

A man feeds swans in the snow

This striking photo from Marcin Ryczek caught our eye recently:

A Man Feeding Swans in the Snow. This photograph was taken in Krakow, from the Grunwald Bridge. The nature-created contrast of white snow and dark water—the two separated by a straight line of the shore—serves as a reflection of the Yin Yang symbol of opposing, yet complementary forces.
