Word of the Day

Slough (n./v., SLEW or SLUFF)

Buckle up kids, we've got a weird one. Slough pronounced like SLEW means a swamp or a state of despair, especially moral lapse. Slough pronounced like SLUFF means an outer layer of skin that's been shed or to shed something like an outer layer of skin. We're not even going to try to give our usual quip or cheeky illustration.
