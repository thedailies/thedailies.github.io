Link of the Day

Alma Haser's puzzle-style portraits of twins

Alma Haser is a German photographer. A significant portion of her work has featured twins. For a recent exhibition, she printed out her photos of twins onto giant puzzles, then painstakingly replaced every other piece so that each portrait has exactly as much of the other twin as it does of the current one.
