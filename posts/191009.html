Word of the Day

Detriment (n., DETT-rih-meant)

Damage, harm or loss, or something that causes that. Unrelated to the remnants of Detlef Schrempf (long live the Seattle Supersonics); Shawn Kemp was the one who did more of the damage (to both teams).
