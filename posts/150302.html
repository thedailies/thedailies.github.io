Word of the Day

Ilunga (n., i-LOON-gah)

Someone who is ready to forgive any abuse for the first time, to tolerate it a second time, but never a third time. In 2004, The word was chosen as the "most untranslatable word." Clearly, none of the 1,000 linguists in the study had ilunga towards ilunga.
