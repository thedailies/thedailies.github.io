Word of the Day

Scrounge (v., SKROUNJ)

It's 2:30 AM. You are hungry. You traipse to the kitchen, shuffling your way to the refrigerator, and grab something. You have just scrounged a meal.

It is morning. People have discovered what you ate. You did not pay for that item. You do not have an intention of paying for it. You are the other defintion of a scrounger, and you are also the worst roommate ever.
