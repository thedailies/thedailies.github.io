Word of the Day

Accoutrement (n., uh-KOO-truh-meant)

An accessory or something that's unnecessary but shows your status, wealth, and knowledge. A Mercedez-Benz. A monogrammed anything. Cufflinks. A receipt from Cold Stone Creamery. Groupies.
