Word of the Day

Postulate (v./n., pahs-CHOO-late)

To assert something, either as truth or as a hypothesis, or something that is set forth as truth. Often used by politicians, a postulate is most frequently deployed in bedtime negotiations.

Link of the Day

Timelapse video of a cloud inversion in the Grand Canyon

Cloud inversions are cool and rare, but we had photos of one a couple years ago and now Harun Mehmedinovic has produced a stunning timelapse video of one—in the Grand Canyon!

This was created as part of the Skyglow Project, which seeks to demonstrate how much light pollution exists in the night skies of major cities. There's lots more at their website.
