Word of the Day

Florilegium (n., FLORE-uh-LEE-gee-umm)

An anthology or a collection of literary excerpts. It comes from terms meaning "flower gathering," so there you have it: flowers = words. Of course, Shakespeare knew that a few hundred years ago.
