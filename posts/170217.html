Word of the Day

Boreal (adj., BORE-ee-ull)

Coming from or relating to the north. Often used in relating to the north wind or the Northern Lights, aurora borealis. A boreal wind and climate is exactly what you think it is.

Link of the Day

Lara Downes - American Again

Lara Downes' recent album, America Again, is an example of how to do an anthology well. It's loosely organized around a theme—Langston Hughes' poem "Let America Be America Again"—and features a wide spectrum of American songs, both traditional and more contemporary. Downes is a pianist, and she plays these songs beautifully, infusing them with enough stateliness to let their timelessness show through but enough pathos to move the soul. Her album demands multiple listens to fully absorb the details. Highly recommended.
