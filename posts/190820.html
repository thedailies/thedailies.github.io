Word of the Day

Acuity (n., uh-CUE-ih-tea)

Sharpness of vision, thought, or other senses. It has been used to refer to acts of physical skill (think gynmastics, which it sounds like it should work for), but it's more in the head. It is not what results from Heady Topper, however.
