Word of the Day

Podunk (n., POE-dunk)

A tiny, unimportant, isolated town in the middle of nowhere. Where Rey is from (according to Rian Johnson, but not the internet). Where a trip to the grocery store is fifteen minutes (just like any store). Where the air is fresh and light pollution is low (and where the sky touches the edge of the world instead of cheating behind a building).
