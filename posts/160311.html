Word of the Day

Convivial (adj., kehn-VIV-ee-ull)

Lively and enjoyable, sociable, or characterized by cheerfulness. Usage note: if someone uses "convivial" in conversation, they are either someone that the word applies perfectly to or a misanthropic writer. (Or they've been hanging around here a lot.)
