Word of the Day

Unsullied (adj., un-SULL-leed)

Unspoiled and pure. Our dreams, not our persons. A team's championship, not its season. A work of art, not the practice. A sunset, not the earth it warms.

Link of the Day

Freeline Skate Tricks

I'm not usually blown away by skate videos and the tricks in them, but this one for freeline skates was very cool.

Freeline skates are basically mini-skateboards for each foot. Each foot gets its own two-wheeled skate, which means that it takes a crazy amount of concentration to keep things together—but also has more freedom to do some things than a regular skateboard would. Take a look:

There's a gracefulness and rhythm to this video that feels very unique to me. Maybe I haven't seen the other videos that have those qualities. But the freeline skate tricks have some interesting creativity to them that's cool to see.
