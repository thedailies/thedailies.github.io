Word of the Day

Refractory (adj., rih-FRACK-tuh-ree)

Difficult to work with, obstinant, resistant to control. Kanye. A greased watermelon in a swimming pool. Some two-year-olds. An overpowered jetpack. Corporate lawyers. A flock of sheep.
