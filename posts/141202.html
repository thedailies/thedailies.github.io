Link of the Day

Paintballing with Hezbollah

Long read of the week, about four journalists, a former Army ranger, and four Hezbollah fighters playing paintball together. Yes, you read that right.

Our collective reasoning for the game was simple: bragging rights. Hezbollah’s military wing is widely considered the most competent group of “nonstate actors”—or, depending where you sit, “terrorists”—in the world....If I could get them into a paintball game, I could witness their battlefield tactics firsthand. And if our team could beat them, we could walk around calling ourselves “the most dangerous nonstate actors on the planet.”
