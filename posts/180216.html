Crokinole

One of our favorite winter Olympic sports is Curling. It's a weird, slow game, very different from the normal rush of Olympic sports, rewarding strategy and fine motor control over brawn, speed, and fancy equipment.

Recently, we found out about a tabletop game called Crokinle, which feels a bit like circular curling, shuffleboard, and marbles all in one. Here's a match between two top players:

If you want to learn the rules of the game, Wikipedia has a decent summary, and this video fills in the rest in three minutes. If you want to play the game, this website has a fun online version.
