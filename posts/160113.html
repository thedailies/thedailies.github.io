Word of the Day

Gamut (n., gahm-UHTT)

The complete range of something, especially a series of notes. This makes no sense because "gamut" only goes from A to T and those two letters are next to each other. Where's the full gamut of letters? Why aren't they in that word? English, you're slipping.
