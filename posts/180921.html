Cachet (n., cah-SHAY)

Musuem Day 2018

Tomorrow, a lot of museums are offering free admission as part of Museum Day. There's a lot of museums and GOOD museums offering this. You can see the full list at Smithsonian Magazine's website, where you can sign up to get your ticket. This is a really cool thing, and we wish we'd gotten it earlier to tell you all. Still: go sign up and go.
