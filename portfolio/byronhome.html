Website Usability Evaluation

Client

  • Byron Home, Inc.

Purpose

  • Website usability testing

Notes

  • Performed traditional web usability test
  • Uncovered six usability problems
  • Discovered how client's target audience actually uses the website

Byron Home, Inc. helps senior citizens downsize and move into retirement or assisted living communities. They were one of the first companies in the field and service a large area of New Jersey.

I conducted a traditional usability test on the Byron Home website, which uncovered six usability problems. The problems were ranked and presented to the president, who appreciated the report and said she would try to implement some of the suggestions.
