Article: The Return

Client

  • American Legion Jersey Boys State

Purpose

  • Feature article for program website

Notes

  • Article ended the 2012 session
  • Pushed website content in a journalistic direction

After nine years as a city counselor at American Legion Jersey Boys State, I was selected as the Digital/Web Copywriter in 2012. Instead of guiding a group of sixty teenagers and four staff as a city counselor, I tried to shed insight into the events, history, and meaning of ALJBS. I published two articles a day: a shorter recap in the morning and a feature in the afternoon/evening. This feature closed the 2012 session. To date, it is my favorite thing that I've written.
