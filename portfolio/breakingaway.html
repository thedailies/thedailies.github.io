Article: Breaking Away

Client

  • The Bucknellian

Purpose

  • Feature article on nationally recognized student cyclist

Notes

  • Profile piece for student newspaper
  • Written under a 700 word limit

Most profile articles have a single focus but Todd Dowling practically refused it. Dowling, given a national award for his work with Bucknell Cycling, emphasized the efforts of his team members in an interview I conducted with him (and his award application). The result was an interesting profile piece, chronicling the rise of the cycling team along with Dowling's accolade.
