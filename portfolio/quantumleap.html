Presentation: The Viability of Natural User Interfaces

Client

  • Spaghetti Automotive Engineering

Purpose

  • Evaluate usefulness of a Natural User Interface device for a business

Notes

  • Wrote research paper, slides
  • Slideshow designed for viewing, not presenting

For this project, I researched whether a Natural User Interface like the Leap would be useful to a traditional business. A 25-page research paper was condensed into a set of slides. Since I would not be able to present the slideshow, I designed the slideshow for online viewing.
