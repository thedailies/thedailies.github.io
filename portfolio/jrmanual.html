John Ryan First Tennessee Bank Install Manual

Client

  • John Ryan

Purpose

  • Create installation manual for digital signage rollout

Notes

  • Manual created in two business days
  • Technical diagrams use a consistent scale

Technical graphics, although often impossible for small companies, are important parts of documentation—especially at a job site. Technicians aren't always able to carefully read a manual on site, making good graphics essential.

On this manual, despite the lack of a graphics program and the tight time window, the graphics turned out well. Created in Microsoft Word but drawn to scale, the graphics show accurate measurements so that technicians could understand them at a quick glance.
