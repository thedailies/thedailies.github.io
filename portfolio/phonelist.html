Rhombus Services Phone List

Client

  • Rhombus Services

Purpose

  • Improve usability of phone list

Notes

  • Made names larger and easier to spot quickly
  • Chunked names into groups

Each person at Rhombus Services answers the phone. Many of these calls are forwarded to another, more appropriate person. The phone list, then, is more than just a record of employees; it is an essential tool.

The Rhombus Services phone list was a mess: a difficult-to-read Google Document in no discernable order. I made the phone list easier to scan in two ways. First, I chunked the names by job function, aligning them into recognizable groupings. Second, I made the names bigger so they could be spotted more easily. The redesign was a success.
