Dodge Dart LED Taillight Installation Instructions

Client

  • Digi-Tails

Purpose

  • Edit and redesign LED taillight installation instructions

Notes

  • Edited wording to fix grammar and tone
  • Redesigned document for scannability and flow
  • Results were praised by company

Digi-Tails is a leading manufacturer of LED taillights for classic cars, with a wide range of available models. Their instruction manuals were designed by another vendor and needed a redesign. I was commissioned to redo this set of instructions and returned a copy to them within one business day. The company was very pleased with the redesign and currently ships this version of the manual with the product.
