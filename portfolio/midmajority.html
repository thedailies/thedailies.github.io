Article: A Song for Every One of Us

Client

  • The Mid-Majority

Purpose

  • Article for notable basketball website

Notes

  • Game recap for college basketball website focusing on small schools
  • Part of the 800 Games Project, which I helped to edit

One of my favorite experiences from the last five years has been reading The Mid-Majority, ostensibly a website about small-school college basketball but ultimately about love for a game, the experience of the game, and fellow travelers.

During the 2011-2012 season, the website became a running log as over 130 people attended and recapped games across the United States. I edited many excellent articles and was able to recap a few, including this recap, focusing on an important Ivy League game.
